Cookie Policy

At TipTheVerse, we use as few cookies as possible — just enough to keep the ripples moving smoothly and to understand how people find us.

1. What Cookies We Use

  • Necessary cookies — Basic site operation (like saving your consent choice) so the banner doesn’t keep popping up.
  • Analytics cookies — Anonymous data that helps us see which pages people visit, so we can improve how the Verse flows. These load only after you click Accept.

2. No Tracking of Personal Data

We don’t sell, trade, or share any personal information. We use Google Analytics 4 with IP anonymization turned on, so we never see who you are — only general activity and region-level data.

3. How to Withdraw or Change Consent

You can clear or reset your cookie choice anytime. In your browser, delete “site data” or run this in the console:

localStorage.removeItem('ttv-consent')

Then refresh the page, and the banner will appear again.

4. Questions?

If you have any questions about privacy or cookies, you can reach out through our contact form or email hello@tiptheverse.com.
