TipTheVerse — Ripples Map
Collective thought has power — see where the ripples are forming.

How it works

Pick a city (and optional state) and tap Drop a Ripple. No names, no tracking — just a city-level pulse. You can also use Click-to-Drop and place a pin anywhere on the map.

This prototype saves pins to your device only (localStorage). If you want everyone to see the same pins, export and wire to a simple backend later.
